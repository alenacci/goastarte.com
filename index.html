From the Sensors to the Cloud:
a Full Stack Solution

Astarte is a system to monitor and remotely control any type of machine connected to the Internet of Things. Astarte enables all the common scenarios for the management and control of the Things, with additional added values ​​such as completely remote management, storage of any flow of data produced by the automation and control systems, quick search for faults, anomalies or outstanding scenarios in the recorded and incoming data, easy, secure and fast addition and removal of Things by the customer herself. Astarte is protected by state of art security practices to allow customers to confidently operate on the instrumented facilities from the internet. Astarte also eliminates the costs related to the management of firmware and hardware through HemeraOS, a multi-platform, multi-operating system architecture ready to deploy on any type of embedded system on the market. HemeraOS allows for a fast and immediate connection every system to the Astarte cloud, for deployment effort and time-to-market reduced to the bare minimum.
