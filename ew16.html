The full stack solution for Industry 4.0

Create powerful, connected and multimedia-enabled industrial applications with Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 and Astarte by Ispirata. In this application, a brushless engine driven by ST’s Nucleo motor driver is controlled through a native iPad application, wirelessly connected to Qualcomm Dragonboard 410c. Data statistics and usage are sent by the Dragonboard to Astarte. Cloud whenever an internet connection is available.

The Dragonboard is running Astarte’s stack on top of Hemera Linux, allowing a secure and reliable connection to both the iPad and the Cloud. Astarte’s technology is designed to put reliability and security before anything else - the iPad is in fact able to speak to the Dragonboard even if no connection is available, whereas the Dragonboard is sending and receiving data from the Cloud whenever the connection is available.

Astarte’s Cloud stack can be deployed on your cloud provider of choice, or even on your own private infrastructure, to make sure you’re in control of your data. It is also designed to be extended with your own web frontend or integrated with your existing business logic, such as ERPs, CRMs, and more.

Qualcomm Snapdragon 410c’s multimedia capabilities are shown in the companion HDMI display: a native Qt5 application is rendered on top of a fully open-source Wayland/DRM graphics stack, meaning awesome performances, high reliability and full GPU offload for processing. Astarte and Qualcomm Snapdragon allow you to create your own IoT/Industry 4.0 Application in the blink of an eye, relying on a stable, maintained and secure stack which is already used by many companies worldwide.
